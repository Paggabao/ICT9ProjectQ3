How to Prevent Fires in Your Home

Introduction

Do Not Leave Your Kitchen Unattended

Unattended cooking is a cause of fires in the kitchen

Don't Use Too Much Wires

When you use too many wires at the same time it will overheat and cause a fire.

Store All Flammable Objects In The Correct Place

If any flammable object gets place in the wrong place it might cause a fire

Remove Lint From Your Dryer Filter After Every Use

Its important to remove the lint from you dryer since it will cause a fire
